We Worship Soup

In Hypixel Skyblock.

The Soup Cult is a skyblock guild that worships and prays to the mushrooms god. If you want to read about the origins of the guild, check out The Mushroom Bible. In The Cult, we worship holy soup, which is made from mushrooms, redstone, and glowstone.

The Cult is structured like a democracy. At the bottom are Soup Trials, who must prove themselves worthy by demostrating their love for soup. Next is Soup Cultist, who prays and serves to the Mushroom God and is a general follower of the soup. Then there is the Soup Council, who votes on actions they think the cult should take. Then you have the Cult Leader who runs the cult and is in charge of the Council. He can veto any decision the Council makes (other than voting off the leader of course). If the Leader can no longer work or if the Council votes him off, then a new Council member would be elected from within the Council. The current leader is Mistere.
